Chai Masala

Boiling Water Recipe Cheese-N'-Crackers

Description

Ingredients

  • 1/4 tsp cardomon
  • 5 peppercorns
  • 3 cloves
  • 1/2 tbsp diced ginger
  • 3 black tea leaves
  • 2 cups of H2o
  • 1 cup milk (your choice of milk type)
  • 3 sticks cinnamon

Steps

  1. Crush the cardomon to release the flavors of the inside of the shell
  2. Add all spices to the boiling water
  3. Continue to boil water and spices
  4. Add milk to boiling spice mixture, simmer and stir for 5 mins
  5. Strain the mixture of the spices, leaving only the mixture
  6. Add brown sugar to taste
  7. Serve and enjoy!
