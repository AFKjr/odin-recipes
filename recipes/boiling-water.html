Boiling Water

Chai Masala Recipe Cheese-N'-Crackers Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 quart of cold or luckwarm H2o
  • Kettle or water heater

Steps

  1. Take the H2o and pour into the water kettle or heater
  2. Boil till boiling hot (212F, or 100C)
  3. Let it cool for 10 mins, serve, drink, and enjoy!
