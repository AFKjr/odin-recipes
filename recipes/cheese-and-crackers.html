Cheese N' Crackers

Chai-Masala Recipe Boiling Water Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 block of Cheese
  • One box of crackers of your choice (I personally enjoy the cheese Ritz crackers)
  • Optional: Salami Deli Meats or you protein of choice

Steps

  1. Cut the block of cheese into slices and place them on the board
  2. Take the crackers out of the package and place them on the board
  3. If you opted for a protein place them on your board
  4. Enjoy separately, or as little sandwiches
